General Services Administration

The General Services Administration (GSA) manages the government's buildings, property, and oversees a large number of government contracts.

Common FOIA requests from GSA include federal contracts, purchase card use, and government forms.

You may not need to make a FOIA request for the information you're looking for. The General Services Administration has:

  • public datasets catalogued on Data.gov
  • public records already released through FOIA
